Xiangshan 象山

Elephant Mountain

The best time to hike the mountain is during late afternoon, this way you can catch the sunset as well as a daytime view and stunning nighttime view of the city, however the trail can get relatively crowded during the weekend. The hike takes a total of around 15-20 minutes to the top, and includes lots of stairs! Remember to take a left at the fork in the path. After around 8-10 minutes, there will be a landing area with facilities and restrooms where the path branches off in multiple directions.

From Taipei 101: From the base of Taipei 101, walk east on Xinyi Road (信義路) for two blocks until you reach MRT Xiangshan Station, only a 15 minute walk from 101 and completely worth it. To get there faster, take a YouBike from the station across the street from 101 (MRT Exit 2), then return the bike at the park nearby the trail entrance.

Pics from my trips

Wuji Tianyuan Temple 淡水天元宮

Was really lucky to be able to still catch the cherry blossoms in mid April! I visited the temple and vicinity at around 3pm+ on a weekday afternoon....I would say the visitor traffic is moderate... with some efforts and patience, it was still possible to take a picture without being photobombed by other visitors.... Took some flights of stairs up the temple to enjoy that peaceful and breathtaking view of the surroundings from the top!

Chang Kai-shek Memorial Hall

中正紀念堂

I am in awe when I saw this vast complex. I am not expecting this especially just around the main city and it's free entrance! The main hall is an interesting architectural piece. Inside and out, from top to bottom - it's really amazing. It housed the big statue of Chiang Kai Shek and there's an hourly ceremony for the changing of guards. You can go there to get a good spot to view the ceremony because it can be too crowded sometimes.

Dahu Park 大湖公園

I have been to Dahu Park several times. The last time I went there was to go fishing ! :o) Catch and release (you definitely don't want to eat those fish...yuck !) The park is a relaxing place to bring the kids...there is a lake in the middle of the whole place, bathrooms,...parking around the edge...
